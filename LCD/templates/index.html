Last Chance Dance is your final opportunity to let that special someone know that you've been interested in them, even if you're been too reserved to 'make a move'. It's now or never baby!

How it works

  1. Sign in with Your Google Account.
  2. Go to Settings and Pair your Google Account with your Carleton Email ID.
  3. Go to Crushes and enter up to five people!
  4. Feel free to update your Crushes until June 7, 2011, when your Crushes will be locked and the system will start to calculate the matches.
  5. A match occurs when one of your crushes also selects you as a crush.
  6. On June 10, 2011 you will receive an automatically generated email with your matches.
