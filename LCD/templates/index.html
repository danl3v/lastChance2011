What is LCD

LCD allows you to discover the people you always wanted to dance with, but never had the chance or were too scared to ask.

How does it work

  1. Sign in with Your Google Account
  2. Pair your Google Account with your Carleton Email ID
  3. Go to the preferences page and enter up to five people!
  4. Feel free to update your preferences until June 7, 2011, when your preferences will be locked and the system will start to calculate the matches
  5. A match occurs when you choose someone and that person also selects you.
  6. On June 10, 2011 you will receive an automatically generated email with your matches.
